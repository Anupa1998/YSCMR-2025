About Conferences

The Young Scientists’ Conference on Multidisciplinary Research (YSCMR), organized by the Young Scientists’ Association of the National Institute of Fundamental Studies (NIFS-YSA), is an international virtual event that brings together young researchers from diverse disciplines. It provides a dynamic platform for undergraduates, postgraduate students and early-career researchers to showcase their innovative work.

Guided by the theme
Scientia et Innovatio: Vision to Venture – Bridging Research, Innovation and Commercialization for a New Global Frontier

The conference welcomes contributions across a broad range of fields:

  • Food Science and Sustainable Agriculture
  • Microbial Systems, Molecular Biotechnology and Natural Product Chemistry
  • Advanced Material Science, Energy Technologies and Quantum Innovations
  • Earth, Environment and Biodiversity
  • Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Mathematical Modelling
